Biography

Shah Rukh Khan is also popularly known as SRK, a famous and popular Bollywood film actor. He is also called the Baadshah of Bollywood. He was born in the year of 2 November 1965. His parents are Taj Mohammad Khan and Lateef Fatheema Khan. He began his career in the late '80s in a television serial named Circus as a child artist, which gave him multiple chances and fame in movies. In Beginning of his career, Shah Rukh Khan has acted in the Television serials Directed and produced by Colonel Raj Kapoor telecasted in Doordarshan in the year 1988. The first serial he acted in was Lekh Tandon's Dil Dariya. In the serial Fauji, SRK has won the audience's hearts as Abhimanyu Rai. But Fauji has telecasted first, and Khan did a tremendous job and grabbed the movie's opportunity. Initially, Shah Rukh Khan used to work for 16 hours per day. Shahrukh Khan acted in his debut movie named Deewana (1992). In his earlier career, he acted like a villain and had negative roles in the movies of Baazigar, Daar, etc. Dilwale Dulhaniye Le Jayenge (1995), also popular as DDLJ, Shah Rukh khan's image has been changed from villain to a romantic hero. Some of the romantic movies such as DDLJ, Kuch Kuch Hota hai (1998), and Kabhi Khushi Kabhi Ghum (2001) are some epic movies that have proved Shahrukh Khan as a romantic hero and grabbed the hearts of youth and made him a trendsetter. Later in the film Devdas (2002), a famous portrayal movie, Shah Rukh Khan has made tremendous performance as an alcoholic and proved his talent. Yash Chopra and Aditya Chopra have seen a romantic actor in Khan and given a chance in a movie named Kuch Kuch Hota hai (1998), which has changed the life of Shahrukh Khan as well as Kajol. These movies have made them a trendsetter, and Khan has acted in seven movies in a year. Hard work has given him a chance to act with Bachchan's family. In Kabhi Khushi Kabhi Ghum (2001), Khan was given equal priority along with Amitabh Bachan and Jaya Bachan by Yash films in which Shah Rukh Khan proved himself again as a romantic hero.Later in a movie called Swadesh (2004) as a NASA Scientist, a man with Asperger syndrome in My Name Is Khan (2010) and in a movied named Chak De India (2007) as a hockey coach for the Indian team. After this, Khan turned to the comedy movies like Chennai Express (2013), Dilwale (2015), and crime movies, and he acted in the movie Raees (2017). After Baadshah (1999) movie, Shah Rukh Khan has always been tagged under the name, Baadshah. Shah Rukh Khan is also titled - The undisputed King of Bollywood. In most of the movies, Shah Rukh Khan used to choose to act in the nationalistic and patriotic movies beyond nationalism, racism, and socialism.
